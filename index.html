My name is Sheldon Cooper and I am a theoretical physicist at the California Institute of Technology. I have a genius-level IQ. Consequently, I have worked on many scientific projects. Instead of discussing these projects, you would probably be more interested in learning about my friends. I have even included another section which lists some of my hobbies. Portfolio

Leonard

Penny

My name is Penny and I am not an egghead like my friends from the California Institute of Technology. However, I have taught my friends a great deal, particularly Sheldon. I moved to California since I am an aspriring actress.

Howard

My name is Howard Wolowitz and I am an engineer at the California Institute of Technology. I may not have a genius-level IQ. However, I am an astronaut and love to irritate Sheldon. My mother has lovable voice that would be a great ring-tone.

Raj

My name is Raj Koothrappali and I am an astophysicist at the California Institute of Technology. I too have a genius-level IQ. I am kind of Howard's side-kick and best friend. I love my dog, which is a little Yorkie. I too like comic books and science fiction.

Amy

My name is Amy Farrah Fowler and I am a neuro-scientist at the California Institute of Technology. I am Sheldon Cooper's girlfriend and eventually we get married. Together, we win the Nobel Prize for our research on super-asymmetric string theory.

Bernadette

My name is Bernadette and I was actually brave enough to marry Howard. I hold a Phd and work in the pharmaceutical industry. I am a busy mother, particularly if you count Howard as one of the children. I met these folks while working at the Cheesecake Factory.
